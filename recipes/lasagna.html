Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an italian dish made of a type of pasta known as lasagne baked with meat or vegetables and a cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Meat
  • Egg
  • Cheese
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • Fresh parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black pepper

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. cook the noodles
  3. prepare the cheese mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
